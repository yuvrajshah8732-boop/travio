# Travio
Travel-Based Social Dating WebApp
In-Depth Concept, Experience Design, Algorithms & Safety Framework
1. Platform Philosophy
This platform is a fun-first, experience-driven social dating webapp built around real travel timelines. Travel is the setting. Social connection is the core. The goal is to make journeys exciting, spontaneous, and socially interactive while maintaining strong safety infrastructure.

Unlike traditional dating apps, this platform reduces endless chatting and increases real-world interaction by grouping users based on shared travel plans.
